Subject: PingSanto Agent – Readiness Metrics Update

Hi,

Readiness telemetry update snapshot:

- New Prometheus series are live in the agent build:
  - `pingsanto_agent_ready_categories_info{category,severity}` tracks current readiness categories with normalized severities.
  - `pingsanto_agent_ready_category_transitions_total{category,severity}` counts ready→not_ready transitions per category/severity pair.
- Taxonomy remains `QUEUE_PRESSURE`, `MONITOR_PENDING`, `MONITOR_STALE`, `MONITOR_ERROR`, `CERT_EXPIRING`, `CERT_EXPIRED` with severities (`warning`, `info`, `warning`, `critical`, `warning`, `critical` respectively).
- Existing metrics (`ready`, `ready_info`, `ready_transitions_total`, `ready_alerts_total`) remain unchanged and compatible with existing dashboards.

Decisions captured:
1. Dashboards can ingest the new category metrics as-is; no additional labels needed today beyond the `{category,severity}` pairs.
2. `ready_alerts_total` plus the per-category counters handle alert dedupe once severity metadata (now emitted) is present.
3. Scrape cadence remains 15 s with no new retention constraints; revisit if future storage limits surface.
